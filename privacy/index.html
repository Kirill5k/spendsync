Introduction

SpendSync ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

The app collects and stores data you enter through the app interface. This includes:

  • Transactions: Details about your incomes and expenses, including amounts, categories, dates, and any notes or tags you provide.

We do not collect any data beyond what you manually input into the app.

How We Use Your Information

The data you provide is used to:

  • Help you manage and track your finances.
  • Store your transaction records locally on your device.
  • Synchronize your transaction records with a remote server for backup and multi-device access.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your data is stored locally on your device and can only be accessed through the app.

Remote Synchronization: When synchronization is enabled, your data is securely transmitted to our remote server using industry-standard encryption.

Data Protection: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all data entered into the app through the app interface.
  • Modify: You can edit any of your transaction data at any time.
  • Delete: You can delete individual transactions or clear all your data from the app.

If you wish to delete your data from the remote server, you may do so through the app settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated Privacy Policy will be available within the app and on our website. Please review it periodically.
